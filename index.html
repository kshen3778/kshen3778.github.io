What is Bezant?

Bezant is a professional design & marketing agency specializing in Initial Coin Offerings and blockchain projects. We work to ensure that you have the proper marketing material for your token sale and help you establish credibility in the online community by publishing carefully written analysis articles on your project.

What we can do for you

  • Slide Decks

    A professionally designed presentation or standalone deck that you can send to investors or your community.

  • Infographics

    A one-pager or graphical document that contains key information on your project which can be put on your website.

  • Articles

    We can write an analysis article highlighting the value of your project. The article will then be shared via LinkedIn, Reddit, and other channels.
